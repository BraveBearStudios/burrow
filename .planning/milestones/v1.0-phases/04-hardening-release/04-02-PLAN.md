<objective>
Close the capacity-under-concurrency gap and prepare the create/stop service for the reconciler.

This plan makes the create path's capacity check + VMID reservation atomic (so two concurrent creates can never both pass a stale node-RAM read and overcommit a node), threads an optional `reason` into `stopWorkspace` so the reconciler's idle auto-stop can emit `workspace.stopped` with `reason: idle`, adds the three reconciler `Settings` keys, and authors the architecture ADR. This is the foundation Plan 01 (reconciler) depends on.

Purpose: Eliminate the unserialized capacity-read race (Pitfall 5) and give the reconciler a guarded `stopWorkspace(reason=...)` to call, while recording the in-process-reconciler + capacity-lock decision per the repo's ADR-per-phase discipline.
Output: An atomic create-path critical section, a `reason`-carrying `stopWorkspace`, three new non-secret Settings keys, a deterministic capacity-race integration test, and ADR-0010.
</objective>
